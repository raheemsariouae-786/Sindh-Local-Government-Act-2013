# Sindh Local Government Knowledge Hub
## Official Roadmap

---

# Vision

To build the most comprehensive digital knowledge platform on the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

This project will provide authentic legal information, explanations, court judgments, notifications, forms, templates, AI tools, and public services in one place.

---

# Mission

Empowering:

- Councillors
- Chairmen
- Lawyers
- Journalists
- Students
- Researchers
- Government Officers
- Citizens

through free and authentic legal information.
