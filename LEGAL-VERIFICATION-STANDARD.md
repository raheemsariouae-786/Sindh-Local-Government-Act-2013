# LEGAL VERIFICATION STANDARD

## Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF)

Version: 1.0

Status: Official Standard

---

# PURPOSE

This standard establishes the official legal verification framework for all legal information published within the Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF).

No legal content shall be published unless verified according to this standard.

---

# OFFICIAL SOURCE HIERARCHY

Every legal statement shall be verified in the following order of priority.

Priority 1
Official Gazette of the Government of Sindh

Priority 2
Official Acts passed by the Provincial Assembly

Priority 3
Constitution of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan

Priority 4
Official Rules

Priority 5
Official Notifications

Priority 6
Official Government Orders

Priority 7
Official Circulars

Priority 8
Judicial Decisions

Priority 9
Official Departmental Manuals

Priority 10
Official RTI Responses

---

# VERIFICATION PRINCIPLE

Every legal statement must be supported by an official source.

No unofficial source may replace an official legal document.

---

# AMENDMENT VERIFICATION

Whenever an amendment exists:

• Original provision shall remain documented.

• Amendment history shall be recorded.

• Effective date shall be recorded.

• Gazette reference shall be recorded.

---

# AI RULE

Artificial Intelligence shall never invent:

• Legal text

• Sections

• Rules

• Notifications

• Case Law

• Government Orders

Whenever evidence is unavailable, the system shall clearly state:

"Official verification pending."

---

# QUALITY CONTROL

Before publication every page must pass:

✓ Source Verification

✓ Amendment Verification

✓ Citation Verification

✓ Formatting Verification

✓ Legal Review

---

# FINAL PRINCIPLE

Accuracy shall always have priority over speed.

Official legal authenticity shall always have priority over AI-generated convenience.
