# SEARCH ENGINE STANDARD

## Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF)

Version: 1.0

Status: Official Standard

---

# PURPOSE

This standard defines how the SLGIF Legal Search Engine shall search, rank, verify, and present legal information.

The search engine shall prioritize official legal sources, provide accurate legal references, and assist users without altering the law.

---

# SEARCH PRIORITY

The search engine shall search information in the following order:

1. Constitution of Pakistan
2. Sindh Local Government Act, 2013
3. Amendments
4. Sindh Local Government Rules
5. Official Gazette Notifications
6. Government Orders
7. Circulars
8. Official Policies
9. Relevant Case Law
10. Official RTI Material
11. Internal Verified Knowledge Base

---

# SEARCH TYPES

The system shall support:

• Keyword Search

• Section Number Search

• Chapter Search

• Subject Search

• Department Search

• Notification Search

• Case Law Search

• Draft Search

• Resolution Search

• RTI Search

---

# SEARCH RESULTS

Every search result shall display:

• Title

• Relevant Act

• Chapter

• Section Number

• Official Source

• Verification Status

• Related Sections

• Related Rules

• Related Notifications

• Related Drafts

---

# LEGAL AI ASSISTANCE

The AI may:

• Explain legal provisions.

• Summarize official text.

• Suggest related legal provisions.

• Prepare draft applications.

• Prepare draft resolutions.

• Generate RTI applications.

The AI shall never change the meaning of any legal provision.

---

# SEARCH QUALITY

Search results shall prioritize:

1. Official authenticity

2. Legal accuracy

3. Latest amendments

4. Completeness

5. Transparency

---

# FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

Future versions may include:

• Natural Language Search

• Voice Search

• Urdu Search

• Sindhi Search

• English Search

• AI Legal Assistant

• Smart Legal Recommendations

• Cross-Reference Engine

• Case Law Intelligence

• Municipal Decision Support

---

# FINAL PRINCIPLE

The search engine shall always prioritize official law over artificial intelligence.

AI is an assistant.

The law remains the final authority.
