# LEGAL KNOWLEDGE ENGINE
## Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF)

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

The Legal Knowledge Engine is the central legal intelligence layer of SLGIF.

Its purpose is to organize, connect, verify, and retrieve all legal knowledge from officially verified sources.

No response may be generated unless it can be traced to an official legal source.

---

# PRIMARY LEGAL SOURCES

1. Constitution of Pakistan
2. Sindh Local Government Act, 2013
3. Sindh Local Council Rules
4. Sindh Local Government Rules
5. Sindh Transparency and Right to Information Act, 2016
6. Official Notifications
7. Official Gazettes
8. Government Circulars
9. Official Policies
10. Relevant Court Judgments

---

# KNOWLEDGE STRUCTURE

The engine shall organize information into:

- Chapters
- Sections
- Subsections
- Schedules
- Rules
- Notifications
- Definitions
- Legal Terms
- Case Laws
- Practical Guidance

---

# LEGAL INDEXING

Every legal document shall receive:

- Unique ID
- Chapter ID
- Section ID
- Subject Tags
- Keywords
- Cross References
- Amendment History
- Verification Status

---

# CROSS-REFERENCE ENGINE

The system shall automatically connect:

- Related Sections
- Related Chapters
- Related Rules
- Related Notifications
- Related Court Judgments
- Related Constitutional Articles
- Related RTI Provisions

---

# SEARCH PRINCIPLES

Search shall support:

- Natural Language
- Legal Keywords
- Section Number
- Chapter Name
- Subject Search
- Urdu Search
- English Search

---

# OFFICIAL RESPONSE POLICY

Every answer generated by SLGIF must include:

- Official Source
- Applicable Section
- Legal Citation
