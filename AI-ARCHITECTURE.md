# AI ARCHITECTURE

## Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF)

Version: 1.0

Status: Official Architecture

---

# PURPOSE

This document defines the official architecture of the Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF).

The system shall function as an AI-powered legal research, drafting, and decision-support platform based exclusively on verified legal sources.

---

# CORE COMPONENTS

1. Legal Knowledge Base

Stores verified Acts, Rules, Notifications, Circulars, Gazette publications, Government Orders, Case Law, and RTI material.

2. Legal Search Engine

Searches official legal sources using keywords, section numbers, subjects, and natural language.

3. AI Legal Assistant

Explains legal provisions without changing their legal meaning.

4. Draft Generator

Generates draft applications, notices, office orders, resolutions, letters, and RTI requests from verified legal sources.

5. Cross-Reference Engine

Automatically links related Sections, Rules, Notifications, Amendments, and Case Law.

6. Amendment Tracker

Maintains the complete legislative history of every provision.

7. Citation Engine

Attaches official legal references to every answer.

8. Verification Engine

Ensures all published content complies with the Legal Verification Standard.

# GUIDING PRINCIPLES

The system shall:

• Prioritize official law.

• Preserve legal accuracy.

• Maintain transparency.

• Record amendment history.

• Never fabricate legal sources.

• Clearly identify unverified information.
