# Chapter Overview

Chapter I establishes the legal foundation of the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

It introduces the Act by defining its official title, territorial extent, commencement, legal definitions, succession of local governments, relationship between Councils and the Provincial Government, and the legal identity of Councils.

This section establishes the legal identity of the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

### Plain Language Explanation

This section simply tells the reader:

- What the law is officially called.
- Where this law applies.
- When this law became effective.

It acts as the legal introduction to the entire Act.

---

### Legal Importance

Section 1 is important because every Act must clearly identify:

- its official legal name;
- its territorial jurisdiction;
- its commencement.

Without these elements, interpretation of legislation becomes difficult.

### Practical Example

If a lawyer, court, government officer or citizen wants to refer to this legislation in an official document, the official title mentioned in this section must be used.

---

### Scope

Every Chapter, every Section and every Schedule derives its legal identity from this provision.
