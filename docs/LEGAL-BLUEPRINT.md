# Purpose

This blueprint defines the complete architecture of the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013 Digital Legal Reference Project.

It serves as the master implementation guide for repository development, legal documentation, future expansion, and long-term maintenance.

---

# Project Vision

To build the most comprehensive, authentic, structured, and publicly accessible digital legal reference system for the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

---

# Core Objectives

- Preserve the official legal text.
- Maintain complete amendment history.
- Document every Chapter.
- Document every Section.
- Record every Rule.
- Record every Government Notification.
- Record relevant Case Law.
- Maintain Constitutional References.
- Support legal research.
- Support public awareness.
- Build a searchable legal knowledge base.

# Verification Policy

Every legal statement shall be verified against official legal sources before publication.

No unofficial legal text shall be treated as authoritative.
