# Purpose

This document establishes the governance framework for the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013 Digital Legal Reference Project.

It defines how documentation shall be created, reviewed, verified, maintained, and published throughout the repository.

Every document contained within this repository shall follow the governance principles defined in this file.

---

# Repository Constitution

This document serves as the constitutional framework of the repository.

All repository standards, documentation policies, verification procedures, editorial rules, and future documentation shall remain consistent with this governance document.

Where a conflict exists between this document and any other repository document, this governance document shall take precedence until formally amended.

---

# Core Governance Principles

The repository shall be governed by the following principles:

1. Official legal sources take precedence over every secondary source.
2. Official Gazette text shall never be modified.
3. Commentary shall always remain separate from official legal text.
4. Every factual claim shall be verifiable.
5. Repository history shall remain transparent.
6. Documentation shall remain modular and maintainable.
7. Every significant change shall be recorded through version control.
8. Repository standards shall remain consistent across all files.
9. Long-term maintainability shall take priority over short-term convenience.
10. Legal neutrality shall be preserved at all times.
