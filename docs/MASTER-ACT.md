# Project Mission

This repository is designed to become the most comprehensive digital legal reference for the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

The project preserves the official legal structure of the Act while providing authenticated legal documentation, amendment tracking, judicial references, practical explanations, and educational material.

No part of the Official Gazette shall be modified.

---

# Documentation Objectives

This repository aims to:

- Preserve the official legal text.
- Document every Chapter.
- Document every Section.
- Record every Amendment.

# Verification Policy

Before any documentation becomes final, it must be verified against:

- Official Gazette
- Official Amendments
- Government Notifications
- Relevant Rules
- Superior Court Judgments
- Constitutional Provisions

Unverified legal text shall never be marked as complete.

---

# Editorial Policy

The following rules are mandatory.

- Official wording shall never be altered.
- Commentary shall remain separate.
- Sources shall always be cited.
- Every amendment shall be recorded separately.
- Every update shall preserve version history.
- Markdown formatting shall remain consistent.
- Duplicate documentation is prohibited.

---

# Amendment Policy

Every amendment shall include:

- Amendment Number
- Amendment Act
- Date of Enforcement
- Affected Sections
- Nature of Amendment
- Official Gazette Citation

# Case Law Policy
