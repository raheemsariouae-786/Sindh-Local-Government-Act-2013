# Contributing to Sindh Local Government Knowledge Hub

Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this public project.

Our goal is to create Pakistan's most authentic and comprehensive digital platform for the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

---

# Who Can Contribute?

Everyone is welcome to contribute, including:

- Councillors
- Lawyers
- Judges
- Government Officers
- Journalists
- Researchers
- Students
- Citizens

---

# Contribution Guidelines

Please ensure that every contribution:

- Is based on authentic legal sources.
- Includes references wherever possible.
- Maintains a respectful and professional tone.
- Avoids political bias.
- Improves public understanding of local government law.

---

# What Can You Contribute?

You may contribute:

- Legal corrections
- Urdu translations
- Sindhi translations
- English improvements
- Court judgments
- Government notifications
- Rules and amendments
- Forms and templates
- Educational content
- Technical improvements
- Bug fixes

---

# Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to:

- Respect others.
- Use professional language.
- Avoid offensive content.
- Focus on public service.
- Support transparency and good governance.

---

# Reporting Issues

If you find:

- Incorrect legal information
- Broken links
- Translation errors
- Missing sections
- Technical bugs

Please create an Issue on GitHub.
