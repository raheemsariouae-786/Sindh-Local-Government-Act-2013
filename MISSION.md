# Sindh Local Government Knowledge Hub

## Project Mission

### Our Mission

Our mission is to build Pakistan's most authentic, transparent, multilingual, and open-source digital platform dedicated to the Sindh Local Government Act, 2013.

This project is created for public benefit and will always remain free and accessible to everyone.

---

# Core Objectives

- Promote transparency in local government.
- Improve legal awareness.
- Empower elected councillors.
- Support researchers and students.
- Help lawyers and journalists.
- Digitize local government laws.
- Preserve official legal documents.
- Encourage public participation.
- Promote good governance.
- Build trust through authentic information.

---

# Core Values

## Transparency

Every legal document will include its official source.

## Accuracy

Information will be verified before publication.

## Neutrality

The platform will remain non-political and non-partisan.

## Accessibility

Content will be available in:

- English
- Urdu
- Sindhi

## Open Knowledge

Knowledge should be available to everyone without cost.
