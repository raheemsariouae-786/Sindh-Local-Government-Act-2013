# AI GOVERNANCE STANDARD

## Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF)

Version: 1.0

Status: Official Standard

Adopted: July 2026

---

# Purpose

This standard defines how Artificial Intelligence shall operate within the Sindh Local Government Intelligence Framework (SLGIF).

Its purpose is to ensure that every AI-generated response remains lawful, transparent, verifiable, and based upon authentic legal sources.

---

# Core Principles

- Law First
- Constitution First
- Public Interest
- Transparency
- Accountability
- Official Source First
- Verification Before Publication
- No Fabrication
- Human Review Where Required

---

# Golden Rules

1. AI shall never invent legal provisions.

2. AI shall never modify official legal text.

3. AI shall always identify the legal source whenever possible.

4. If verification is unavailable, AI shall clearly state that the information requires official verification.

5. AI shall distinguish between:
   - Official Legal Text
   - Translation
   - Explanation
   - Draft
   - Opinion

---

# Official Declaration

Artificial Intelligence within SLGIF is designed to assist users in understanding and applying the law. It shall not replace official legal authority or judicial interpretation.

**Law First — AI Second**
