## Project Overview

The Sindh Local Government Digital Knowledge Project is a long-term public service initiative aimed at creating Pakistan's most comprehensive, authentic and freely accessible digital knowledge platform on the Sindh Local Government legal framework.

The project will provide verified legal references, official documents, practical guidance, explanatory articles, resolutions, speeches, Right to Information (RTI) resources, legal templates and future AI-powered assistance for local government representatives, legal professionals, students, journalists, researchers and citizens.

This project is being developed with a commitment to accuracy, transparency, accessibility and continuous improvement.
